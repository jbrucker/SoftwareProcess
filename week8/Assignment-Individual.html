Individual Assignment on Software Process Knowledge

Create 4 questions with answers for process-related knowledge that software engineering students (and software engineers) should know. Questions can be anything related to software development process, including specific practices, process areas, and goals.
At least 2 of the questions should be related to your role on the class team project. If you have two roles, such as developer and CM, then 2 questions should be related to your non-developer role such as CM.

The other two questions can be any process-related knowledge that is useful or important for a software engineering to know.

Guidelines for questions and answers:

  1. Questions should be something that demonstrates understanding of good software process, practices, and/or good project management, but they don't need to be complicated or advanced.
  2. A question can ask for a short answer, be multiple choice, or ask to identify something. A question can also be a scenario, asking what to do in a particular project context.
  3. The question should be answerable in at most 1-2 paragraphs, and the answer can be objectively evaluated as correct or not. In general, short answers are better.
  4. For each question, include your answer. You may provide additional explanation of the answer.
  5. For each question provide at least one reference for additional information. Reference can be a URL, an article, or a book. For articles and books, state precisely where to find the information, e.g.
    Jalote, Software Project Management in Practice, section 6.3.
How to submit:
  • Submit questions and answers in a file. If your question and answer are plain text, use plain text file. If you need some formatting, use HTML or Markdown, but no CSS or Javascript.
    You can include images for diagrams. Please upload as separate files. OK to submit a ZIP or RAR archive of files.
  • Include your name in the questions and answers file.
  • Upload your file(s) to LMS.ku.ac.th, the Week 8 assignment. (Probably what you are reading now.)
Evaluation
  • Your score will be based on the quality of the question and answer, its relevance to practice software process knowledge, and correct use of English.
  • The best questions will be included in the midterm. If one of your questions is included, then you should be easy points on the midterm!

Upload a file or files for your questions to LMS.ku.ac.th (week 8), and include your name in the questions file.
