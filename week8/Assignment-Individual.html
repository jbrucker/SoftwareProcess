Individual Assignment on Software Process Knowledge

Create 4 questions with answers for process-related knowledge that software engineering students (and software engineers) should know. Questions can be anything related to software development process, including specific practices, process areas, and goals.
At least 2 of the questions should be related to your role on the class team project. If you have two roles, such as developer and CM, then 2 questions should be related to your non-developer role such as CM.

The other two questions can be any process-related knowledge that is useful or important for a software engineering to know.

Guidelines for questions and answers:

  1. Questions don't need to be complicated, but should be something that demonstrates understanding of good software process, practices, and/or good project management.
  2. A question can be given as a scenario, asking what to do in a given project context or to analyse some context.
  3. The question should be answerable in at most 1-2 paragraphs; short answers are better. The question should be such that an answer can be objectively evaluated as correct or not.
  4. For each question, include your answer. You may provide additional explanation of the answer.
  5. For each question provide at least one reference for additional information. Reference can be a URL, an article, or a book. For articles and books, state precisely where to find the information, e.g.
    Jalote, Software Project Management in Practice, section 6.3.
  6. Submit questions and answers in a file. If your question and answer are plain text, use plain text file. If you need some formatting, use HTML or Markdown, but no CSS or Javascript.
    You can include images for diagrams.
  7. Your questions, answers, and references will be posted online for the entire class to study.
  9. Your score will be based on the quality of the question and answer, its relevance to practice software process knowledge, and correct use of English.

Upload a file or files for your questions to LMS.ku.ac.th (week 8). Include your name in each file (except images).
