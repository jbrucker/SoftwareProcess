Individual Assignment

Create 4 questions with answers for process-related knowledge that software engineering students (and software engineers) should know. At least 2 of the questions should be related to your role on the class team project. If you have two roles, such as developer and CM, then 2 questions should be related to your non-developer role.

Guidelines for questions and answers:

  1. Questions don't have to be complicated, but preferrably be something that demonstrates understanding of good software practices and/or good project management.
  2. A question can be given as a scenario, asking what to do in a given project context or to analyse some context.
  3. For each question, include a clear answer. You may also include additional explanation of the answer.
  4. For each question provide a reference for additional information. Reference can be a URL, an article, or a book. For articles and books, state precisely where to find the information, e.g.
    Jalote, Software Project Management in Practice, section 6.3.
  5. Submit questions as plain text or using markdown.
